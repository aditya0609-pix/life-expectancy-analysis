What is the impact of diseases on Life Expectancy?

Explanation

  • Diseases are the major factor that affect the life expectancy of a country. The diseases that we have analyzed are Aids and measles. These significant effect on the life expectancy of the countries. Aids affect the adult mortality whereas measles affect the infant deaths. The disease can be prevented and treated but still it affects the life expectancy of modern generation. It is due to the lack of efficient health care in some countries. Developed countries have better life expectancy and they have low no. of cases of such disease because they are spending more money on the health care facility of the country.
  • Some of the facts about these diseases like Swaziland has the highest number of cases of aids and Cook Islands has lowest number of case. As for measles china has highest number of cases and Uruguay has the lowest, As mentioned above expenditure on health care affects the number of cases of these diseases. It has direct relation with percentage expenditure of a country. When a country spends more money on its health care facility and have better health care facility it has better life expectancy whereas it is not the case for the countries with lower GDP. These developing are not able to spend more money on their health care facility which increase the number of cases of such diseases.
