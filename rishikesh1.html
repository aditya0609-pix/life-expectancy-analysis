Does immunization affect life expectancy? What are the factors that affect immunization facility of a country?

Explanation

  • Immunization plays a major role in the health of the people of a country. From the analysis and the data from the dataset, it shows the immunization has a effect in the life expectancy. The countries with better immunization facilities have a better life expectancy when compared to the countries with lower immunization facility. The countries with better immunization facilities are mostly the developed nations. The factors that affect the immunization facility of a country is the percentage of expenditure on the field of health care.
  • The immunization of diseases like aids and measles are greatly affected by the expenditure of health care of a country. Generally, countries spend less in the field of health care because, their overall GDP is low, which makes it hard to give better health care to their citizens. The quality of immunization of a country decreases the health of the people is greatly affect which makes a significant difference in the life expectancy of the particular country.
